  • Hey! Come get to know me ✦

    Helloo! I’m Kat, or Tao, a Chinese Canadian UX/UI designer and illustrator from the IDEA School of Design program at Capilano University in Vancouver. Creating things has been a passion following me constantly through the years; whether that be through innovative graphic design or crafting personal creations like digital art, painting, or music. Solving problems with an out-of-the-box, open mindset is what ignites a spark in me to pursue this career.

    There are many things I take interest in, however most of all I love video games and participating in fandom spaces through art expression; both online through the alias ghosttaal and in-person at fan conventions. Within these spaces, I’ve created marketable merchandise and contributed in fan zines to grow a name for myself.

    As long as I am approached with an open mindset and friendly attitude, I will be more than willing to chat about design, hobbies, or anything interesting.

    Reach out to me in whatever way suits you best ✦
