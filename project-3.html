Scarameow's Adventures is a for-profit, digitally downloadable fan project featuring a 2025 themed calendar, desktop & phone wallpapers, a selection of phone app icons, sticker sheets, a shimeji pet, emotes, and a papercraft. All money made from this project was distributed between all the artists working on it to compensate them for their work.

This project is for the fanbase of the Chinese video game Genshin Impact, circulating around the character Scaramouche/Wanderer and the feline version(s) of himself. Scarameow's Adventures is entirely fan-made, the team built from a group of artists who all mutually care for the game and the character.

The project started in June 2024 with the Ko-Fi shop opening in December 2024, later closing on Janurary 3rd, 2025. Between these time periods, our team worked diligently to create beautiful illustrations & merchandise, and promote our project through social media.

Please note that the full calendar & merch cannot be shown due to the project being for-profit, and to respect the artists for their hard work.

Project Contents

Calendar

Scarameow's Adventures' biggest aspect was its 2025 calendar. 12 contributers came together to create beautifully unique pieces to make it come to life.

Other Merch

Alongside the calendar, the fan-project also has a shimeji pet, profile icons & banners, custom app icons & widgets, and mobile & desktop wallpapers. We had a stretch goal for those who purchase the full bundle — by reaching 50 sales, customers could unlock a papercraft, sticker sheets, and emotes too.

Reflection

This being the first ever collaborative fan project I've contributed in, I had an amazing and fruitful experience working with fellow artists & illustrators who shared a passion with me. Not only was I able to develop my graphic design & creative abilities; I had the opportunity to learn what it's like to work in a small business, creating promotional material to make a name for yourself.

This experience was incredibly memorable for me, and it has given me the desire to continue to participate in these spaces, maybe even eventually kickstarting one on my own.
