SCARAMEOW'S ADVENTURES CALENDAR FANZINE

BIOTIFY is a mobile application intended to assist users by guiding them while they are out and about locally in British Columbia. With its map system, camera function, and organizable photo & video library, the service aims to support Vancouver residents who wish to learn more about their surroundings, making nature exploration simple and accessible.

This service was made for my IXD Visual Design 103 course, with the intention to brainstorm and design a mobile application UI that serves users under a certain design domain. The design domain chosen was Nature Exploration. The application has multiple features, acting as a general hub for nature exploration which allows users’ saved information to be well organized and easily accessible all in one singular app.

With British Columbia’s natural beauty being so vast, outdoorsy people can feel overwhelmed by the influx of unlimited possibilities. Such information may be inaccessible, difficult to find, or even inaccurate, which can lead to confusion and lack of cohesion.
